I am a research scholar at the Organic Nano electronics (ONe) Group. My research focuses on the development of stable and efficient perovskite solar cells on flexible substrates. Emphasis is on perovskite layer optimization, and interface engineering by incorporating two-dimensional (2D) materials. Several choice of polymer substrates coated with a conducting layer will be explored.
